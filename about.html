About Us

Our Story

Located in the heart of Kathmandu, TimberArt Nepal brings you unique handmade crafts that showcase the rich heritage of Nepali craftsmanship. Our artisans are skilled in creating intricate wood carvings, traditional Thangka paintings, detailed metal statues, and vibrant scarves, among other items.

Our mission is to preserve the rich tradition of handmade Nepali crafts and make these pieces available to the world. We empower local artisans by promoting their craftsmanship and connecting them with customers across the globe.
